WELCOME TO GWALIOR HOTELS



A hotel is an establishment that provides paid lodging on a short-term basis. Facilities provided inside a hotel room may range from a modest-quality mattress in a small room to large suites with bigger, higher-quality beds, a dresser, a refrigerator and other kitchen facilities, upholstered chairs, a flat screen television, and en-suite bathrooms. Small, lower-priced hotels may offer only the most basic guest services and facilities. Larger, higher-priced hotels may provide additional guest facilities such as a swimming pool, business centre (with computers, printers, and other office equipment), childcare, conference and event facilities, tennis or basketball courts, gymnasium, restaurants, day spa, and social function services. Hotel rooms are usually numbered (or named in some smaller hotels and B&Bs) to allow guests to identify their room. Some boutique, high-end hotels have custom decorated rooms. Some hotels offer meals as part of a room and board arrangement. In the United Kingdom, a hotel is required by law to serve food and drinks to all guests within certain stated hours.[citation needed] In Japan, capsule hotels provide a tiny room suitable only for sleeping and shared bathroom facilities.

BEST HOTELS IN GWALIOR




1.Taj Usha Kiran Palace



A great hotel to enjoy a royal experience, Taj Usha Kiran is one of the best 5-star hotels in Gwalior. Located in the heart of the city, Taj Usha is a 120-year-old palace which has managed to retain its old royal charm. You can see the palace’s rich history reflected through its art and architecture.
Tariff per night: INR 7000 onwards
What’s special: All the rooms are tastefully decorated and offer views of the pool and beautiful gardens. The hotel is in close proximity to popular tourist spots such as the Scindia museum and the Jai Vilas Palace. The Ayurveda Spa offers plenty of therapies for rejuvenation. The hotel also organizes several activities for children and adults.

2. Clarks Inn Suites



A beautiful boutique hotel located near to Gwalior Fort, Clarks Inn Suites is among the best hotels in Gwalior. Its closeness to the airport, railway station and bus depot makes Clark Inn Suites a great choice for business travellers and budget travellers. Though lacking in grand interiors and exteriors, the hotel does offer all modern facilities and services of a five-star hotel at affordable rates.
Tariff per night: INR 4800 onwards
What’s special: This is an all-suite hotel. The royal suites offer fort views and are equipped with a private terrace and separate sitting room, Jacuzzi and shower cubicle.

3. Hotel Neemrana’s Deo Bagh



Set in a sprawling 5-acre heritage site, the hotel offers peace and tranquillity from the hustle and bustle of the city (though the vibrant city is only 5 minutes away!). Surrounded with gardens and fruit orchards, it’s one of the best hotels in Gwalior for nature lovers.
Tariff per night – INR 4500 onwards
What’s special – The hotel houses only 15 rooms all of which are an epitome of luxury. The remaining part of the property has been covered by beautiful garden and orchards which is home to exotic bird species.

4. Radisson Gwalior



Radisson needs no introduction. This is a perfect base for travellers who are seeking a cosy and classy accommodation. Elegantly built and furnished interiors combined with world-class hospitality, it is among the most popular 5-star hotels in Gwalior that make a perfect recipe for a royal holiday.
Tariff per night – INR 4000 onwards
What’s special – Lavishly spread buffet, great ambience and spacious parking lots.

5. Gwalior Regency



Another fabulous hotel located in the heart of the city, Gwalior Regency is one of the finest budget hotels in Gwalior. As with most hotels in Gwalior, it too showcases the city’s heritage culture through its decor and architecture. Travellers can get a taste of the hotel’s fine luxury in the Grand and Grand executive suites.

Tariff per night: INR 2500 onwards
What’s special: Eatology, the restaurant offers a wide range of delicious native dishes. Guests can also enjoy their favourite cocktails in an entertaining ambience at the HQ Lounge.

6. Adityaz



Among the best hotels in Gwalior near the railway station, Adityaz hotel promises a comfortable and warm stay at an affordable rate. It is located in a serene neighbourhood and occasionally the hotel hosts entertainments like musical nights.
Tariff per night – INR 2500 onwards
What’s special – The hotel comes with a discotheque – Spectrum

7. The Central Park Hotel



One of the top names on the list of 3 star hotel in Gwalior, The Central Park Hotel offers an extravagant stay. Rooms are split into three sections – Executive Club Lounge, Semi Club Lounge and Deluxe Suite. The interiors have a beautiful colour assemblage and display a contemporary decor.

Tariff per night – INR 2300 onwards
What’s special – Conference rooms, wedding halls, gym, multi-cuisine restaurant and a bar!

8. MK Vivanta



Surrounded by beautiful landscapes the hotel is pleasant on the eyes. The hotel is located near to the railway station and airport. The architecture stays true to the city’s heritage background. MK Vivanta is probably among the best budget hotels in Gwalior.
Tariff per night – INR 2000 onwards
What’s special – Good location and large rooms

9.Grace



If you’re looking for hotels in Gwalior near the railway station, Grace feels like a home away from home. Housing 18 decent and comfortable rooms categorized in deluxe, executive and suites, it is strategically located only half kilometre away from Gwalior railway station.
Tariff per night – INR 1300 onwards
What’s special – Proximity to tourist hotspots and pick up and drop facilities.

10. Hotel Royal Inn



One of the cheap hotels in Gwalior near the railway station, Hotel Royal Inn is also in close proximity to popular tourist attractions like Sun Temple, Gujari Mahal, Gwalior Fort and Museum. Hotel Royal Inn is suitable for romantic getaways or business stays for those in search of cheap hotels in Gwalior.
Tariff per night – INR 1250 onwards
What’s special – Business centres, pick up and drops. Sightseeing trips and guides (chargeable)

2.Captain’s Cafe





A one of its kind vintage café in Gwalior, Captain’s Café is one of the favorite places where the youth of the city prefer to dine. Continental is the prime cuisine served here, along with different types of teas and coffees. While you’re here with your gang, do try the yummy grilled fish. There’s a special ‘helmet coffee’ which is offered to whoever comes wearing a helmet. Also, the menu is done in the form of a newspaper, which is a unique concept for cafes in Gwalior.

3.Alfanzo Restaurant




A haven for those who stay away from meat, this is the best veg restaurant in Gwalior. This multi-cuisine restaurant has three floors, so seating space is not a problem. While the first floor is a dedicated bakery with a casual seating area, the second floor is a proper dine-in hall, ideal for large groups and families.
The third floor is where you can host your parties because it is a full-fledged banquet hall. An all-rounder when it comes to restaurants, ain’t it? So whether you love proper North Indian fare or would simply like to have a cup of coffee with a muffin, this place has it all.

For authentic Punjabi and North Indian food, you should definitely dine at Shere Punjab Restaurant. It is a popular family restaurant in Gwalior, known for the best chhole-rice in the city.
