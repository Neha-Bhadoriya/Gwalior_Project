FESTIVALS IN GWALIOR


Events and Festivals in Gwalior. Festival: Gwalior's residents celebrate every national festival like Diwali, Id, Christmas, etc with a lot of gaiety and fanfare. But there are certain festivals like Rang Panchami, GaneshChaturthi and Makar Sankrant that occupy a special place among festivals celebrated in Gwalior.

Diwali Festival


In the month of November, Diwali, the festival of lights is celebrated with great fanfare. A day after Diwali, the Baredi dance is performed by male dancers of Ahir community from Bundelkhand on the legend of Krishna. Interestingly, the dance continues for a fortnight, till the full moon night.

Holi Festival


A huge festival of colors - Holi in Gwalior is a great great deal. It is too much fun to dance to loud Bollywood tunes and throw colors and water balloons at each other. ... Nowadays eco-friendly is the new thing; Holi with organic colors and no use of water is the whole concept.
